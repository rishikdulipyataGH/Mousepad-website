About Elite5

Minimal visuals. Max performance.

Elite5 is an independent surface studio building custom pads with OEM partners in China. Every release starts with glide targets, not logos. We obsess over weave density, edge finishes, and base compression. That means a premium pad that vanishes visually yet stays dialed under pressure.
